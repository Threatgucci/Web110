Essential Gear

A few basic items every angler should have:

  • Rod and reel (matched to target species)
  • Line (test rating appropriate for fish size)
  • A small tackle box with hooks, weights, and lures
  • Fishing backpack - Durable bag designed to carry and organize fishing gear conveniently
  • Filleting knife - Specialized knife with a sharp blade for filleting and skinning fish effciently

Important Knots

  1. Improved Clinch Knot - For tying line to hook
  2. Palomar Knot — Strong and simple
  3. Loop Knot — When you want lure freedom
  4. Fisherman knot - joins two lines together, A reliable knot that is easy to tie and provides a strong hold, especially for securing a fishing line to a hook or lure
  5. Palomar knot, Attaches a fishing line to hooks, lures, and swivels.

Safety & Etiquette

Always carry a life jacket if boating, know local regulations, and follow catch-and-release best practices when appropriate.

Local Spots

  • Truman Lake — Easy Shore Access
  • Kansas River — Good for Catfish
  • Hildale Lake — Quiet and Beautiful
  • Black Hoof Park - One of the best reviewed fishing spots around the web
  • Kill Creep Park - People love this park and it's great for kayaking
