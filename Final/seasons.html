Spring

Fish become more active as the water warms. Bass move into shallow water to feed and prepare for spawning. Crappie fishing is excellent along shorelines.

  • Best Baits: Lures, Live Bait, and Jigs
  • Where: Shallow coves, warm water pockets

Summer

Fish feed early morning and late evening. Midday heat pushes many species into deeper, cooler water.

  • Best Baits: Topwater lures early/late, deep crankbaits mid-day
  • Where: Weed edges, shade, deep drop-offs

Fall

One of the best fishing seasons. Fish feed heavily to prepare for winter. Bass and walleye move in schools chasing baitfish.

  • Best Baits: Spinnerbaits, swimbaits
  • Where: Points, banks, creek channels

Winter

Fish are sluggish in cold water and stay near the bottom. Slow presentations work best.

  • Best Baits: Small jigs, live bait
  • Where: Deep holes, slow-moving water
