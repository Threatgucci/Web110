Top 5 Quick Tips

  1. Match lure size to local baitfish.
  2. Fish early morning or late evening in summer.
  3. Have fun, and spend time with the family.
  4. Practice knot-tying until your hands do it automatically.
  5. Respect local rules and fellow anglers.
