Why Safety Matters

Fishing is relaxing and fun, but preparation is key to avoiding injuries and emergencies. Whether you're on a boat, at the bank, or wading, having the right safety knowledge keeps you confident and focused on catching fish.

Even experienced anglers occasionally forget essential items, so using a checklist ensures every trip starts the right way. Consistency helps build smart habits and prevents accidents caused by weather, water conditions, or gear failure.

This page includes a complete safety checklist, must‑have gear, and basic tips every angler should follow—especially beginners.

Essential Safety Checklist

  • Life jacket or PFD (required for boating)
  • First‑aid kit
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Plenty of drinking water
  • Weather‑appropriate clothing

Use this simple list before each fishing outing. Weather, location, and experience level should determine what additional safety items you bring.
